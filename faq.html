Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

We are industry professionals coming together to help celebrate hosts during those hard times. We also hope to bring transparency, giving the keys to all players to better understand our sector.

What do we do?

We aggregate publicly available data from Airbnb and put it together in a ranking format.

How does the ranking work?

With the public data we aggregate, we put together for every host an average review count and score. We then took the top 20 worldwide and for specific countries (United-states, France, Spain and Italy.). We ranked hosts with the highest review score out of 5, then with the highest number of reviews.

How does registration work?

Registration will be available when the worldwide ranking is available later in February. If you register, you will be able to connect with all the top ranked hosts in your market through our platform. We will check the data from your host profile (review score, number of reviews and count of listings) and update them on our website. As a plus, you will be highlighted in the rankings of your city and country. Right now, you can already pre-register to be the first to connect with all the top ranked hosts in your market once we go live globally.

When can we expect worldwide rankings to be available?

We are as excited to you to share rankings of hosts in cities and countries around the world. We are expecting to release globally around mid-February! Be sure to Register now to pre-register and notified when we go live!
